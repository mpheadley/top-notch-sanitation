FAQ

Common Questions

How much does service cost?

Our pricing is straightforward and affordable. Call us at (678) 685-4554 for a quote — setup takes about 5 minutes.

When is my trash picked up?

You'll get a set pickup day each week. Just place your can curbside the night before, and we'll take care of the rest.

What if I have extra trash one week?

No big deal — an extra bag or two is fine. If you're regularly producing more, call us and we'll set you up with a second can.

Do you pick up furniture or large items?

Yes — call the office and we'll quote a special pickup. Most items can be scheduled within a few days.

How do I pay my bill?

Pay online at trashbilling.com, set up Auto Pay, pay by phone at (678) 685-4554, or mail to P.O. Box 975, Rockmart, GA 30153.

What can't go in the trash can?

No hot items (ashes, fireworks), no oil containers, no tires, and no construction materials. Yard waste is picked up separately — call to schedule. If you're unsure about something, just ask.
