Our Story

Started With a Truck and a Handshake

We started Top Notch because Polk County deserved better than missed pickups and unanswered phones.

What began as a small operation serving a handful of Rockmart streets has grown into the go-to trash service for hundreds of households across Polk County. The company is still family-owned and still locally operated.

In a business where the big corporate haulers treat you like an account number, Top Notch treats you like a neighbor — because that's exactly what you are.
