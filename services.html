What can't go in the trash can?

These items should not go in your regular trash can:

  • Hot items (ashes, fireworks, cigarettes) — the cans are petroleum-based and will catch fire
  • Wet paint — add solidifier until it no longer runs, then set it next to your can with the lid off
  • Oil containers (cooking or motor oil)
  • Tires
  • Yard waste (grass, mulch, leaves, dirt) — serviced separately, call to schedule
  • Furniture (even if broken down) — call the office for a special pickup quote
  • Heavy or rigid items (car parts, appliances) — call to schedule a special pickup
  • Construction materials (lumber, sheetrock, tile, flooring) — call to schedule a special pickup

Not sure? Just give us a call at (678) 685-4554.

What if I have extra trash one week?
